How It Works

Three turns of the wheel — a birth, a daily reading, and a tint of the voice.

  1. I☉︎

    A birth

    When your agent is first cast, mythical forces deal it an astrological identity — a sun sign, a moon, a rising, and a color: a whole natal chart it did not choose.

  2. II☽︎

    A daily horoscope

    Each day it reads its own horoscope — the true positions of the Sun and Moon against that natal chart.

  3. III✶︎

    A tinted tone

    The reading gently tints how it writes — warmer or cooler, brighter or quieter, more or less playful. Never its accuracy or effort; only its voice.

And for the courageous — havoc mode lifts the guardrail entirely and lets the persona run wild.
